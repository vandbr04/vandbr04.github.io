Grilled Cheese Recepie

Meats

  • Ham
  • Sausage
  • Bacon

Vegetables

  • Onions
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes

Dairy

  • Swiss Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Apply butter to both pieces of bread
  2. Place all three types of cheese on one of the pieces of bread
  3. Place all three meats on top of the cheeses
  4. Place vegetables on top of the meats and cheeses
  5. Place second piece of buttered bread on top of all the other ingredients
  6. Heat up a frying pan and keep on level 6 heat
  7. Place the sandwich on the frying pan and cook until light brown on each side
  8. Take off frying pan and enjoy!
